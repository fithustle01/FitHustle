💪 PUSH-UP TECHNIQUE

The push-up is king of upper body exercises. It builds chest, triceps and shoulders simultaneously. Master the form first, then increase reps.

  • 1.Place hands slightly wider than shoulder-width
  • 2.Keep body in a straight line — head to heels
  • 3.Lower chest all the way to ground slowly
  • 4.Push back up fully — do not lock elbows
  • 5.Breathe in going down, breathe out going up
  • 6.Start: 3 sets of 10 reps, rest 60 seconds

🏋️ PULL-UP TECHNIQUE

Pull-ups build back, biceps and grip strength. One of the most effective bodyweight exercises for building a V-shaped physique.

  • 1.Grip the bar shoulder-width — overhand grip
  • 2.Hang fully — let shoulders stretch completely
  • 3.Pull chest toward bar — chin above it
  • 4.Squeeze back muscles hard at the top
  • 5.Lower slowly in 2-3 seconds — full control
  • 6.Start: 3 sets of 5-8 reps

🦵 SQUAT TECHNIQUE

Squats are the king of all exercises. They work legs, glutes and core. Proper form prevents knee injuries and maximizes results.

  • 1.Stand feet shoulder-width — toes slightly outward
  • 2.Keep chest up and back straight throughout
  • 3.Push knees in direction of your toes
  • 4.Lower until thighs are parallel to floor
  • 5.Drive through heels to stand back up
  • 6.Start: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

🔥 LEG RAISES TECHNIQUE

Best exercise for lower abs. Strengthens hip flexors and improves core stability. No equipment needed — do it anywhere!

  • 1.Lie flat — hands under your glutes for support
  • 2.Keep legs straight — slight knee bend is okay
  • 3.Slowly raise legs up to 90 degrees
  • 4.Keep lower back pressed to the floor always
  • 5.Lower legs slowly — do NOT touch the floor
  • 6.Start: 3 sets of 12 reps

🏋️ DUMBBELL WORKOUT

Dumbbells let you train every single muscle group. Bicep curls to shoulder press — dumbbells are your best fitness investment.

  • 1.Choose weight you can do 10-12 reps with good form
  • 2.Keep core tight during every exercise
  • 3.Control movement — slow on the way down
  • 4.Full range of motion — squeeze muscle at peak
  • 5.Rest 45-60 seconds between sets
  • 6.Progress by adding weight every 2 weeks

🧱 PLANK TECHNIQUE

The ultimate core exercise. Strengthens abs, back, shoulders and glutes all at once. Just 60 seconds daily makes a huge difference!

  • 1.Start on forearms and toes — elbows under shoulders
  • 2.Body in one straight line — head to heels
  • 3.Do NOT let hips sag or raise up
  • 4.Squeeze core and glutes tight throughout
  • 5.Breathe normally — focus on holding the form
  • 6.Start: 30 seconds — add 5 seconds daily
